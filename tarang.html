ABOUT TARANG

We love music

Tarang – a musical extravaganza from the Indian heartland and its innumerably diverse facets. Cover an existing piece, or create your own. Come as a band and the stage is yours. Registration • Pre-registration (includes video submission). Team participation. • Submit a 2-3 minute long video showcasing your team’s practice routine/previous performances to events@bits-oasis.org and cc the same to pcr@bits-oasis.org. • “ - Oasis ’17 Video Submission”. • For last date for submission, check registration details. • The selected teams, once on-campus should report to the Department of Theatre room (2214, FD-II) with their IDs post registration at the Controls booth.

RULES

No fun without rules

  • Bands can perform any songs of their choice, cover or an original composition.

  • Only two bands per college are allowed with a maximum of 10 members and minimum of 3 members per band. The two teams could have a maximum of 3 common band members.

  • Total Time Limit: 20 minutes (5 minutes for the sound check and 15 minutes for the performance.) If a team exceeds the time allocated for sound check, the extra time taken shall be reduced from the performance time. An indicative announcement will be made at the time limit of 20 minutes to notify the judges about the extension.

  • 5% of the total marks would be deducted for every extra minute. Sounds would be cut after 23 minutes.

  • Use of pre-recorded music (e.g., sequences, keyboard samples, guitar/octapad loops) will result in disqualification.

  • The songs should have lyrics from any Indian language. However, a refrain with English lyrics is allowed.

  • Purely classical numbers are not allowed. However, a classical refrain is allowed

  • Bands should bring their own instruments. Drum kit and sounds equipment will be provided by BITS Pilani.

    Judging Criteria

  • Creativity

  • Quality of vocals and instrumental music

  • Tightness and synchronization

  • Choice of song (in case of cover; composition otherwise)

  • Overall impact
